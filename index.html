Short Bio

He is a research assistant and PhD Candidate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at Virginia Tech university. He recevied his master degree in Computer Engineering from Iran University of Science and Technology (IUST) under supervision of Prof. Mohsen Sharifi in the area of distributed systems. He graduated from Azad University Central Tehran Branch with B.S. in Computer Engineering.

Reserach Area

Currently he is doing his research in Vtess group under supervison of Dr. Huaicheng Li and is co-advised by Dr. Changwoo Min in the area of Operating System OS-level memory management and emerging memory technologies like CXL memory.
