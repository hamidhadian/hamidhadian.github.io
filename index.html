Short Bio

Is a research assistant and PhD student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at Virginia Tech university. He recevied his masters degree in Computer Engineering from Iran University of Science and Technology (IUST) under supervision of Prof Mohsen Sharifi and graduated from Azad University Central Tehran Branch with B.S. in Computer Engineering.

Reserach Area

He is doing his research in Vtess group under supervison of Dr. Huaicheng Li and Dr. Changwoo Min in the area of Operating System OS-level memory management and emerging memory technologies like CXL memory
