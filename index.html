Short Bio

He is a research assistant and Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at Virginia Tech. He received his master's degree in Computer Engineering from Iran University of Science and Technology (IUST) under the supervision of Prof. Mohsen Sharifi in the area of distributed systems.

Research Area

Currently, he is conducting research in the MoatLab group under the supervision of Prof. Huaicheng Li in operating systems—OS-level memory management and emerging memory technologies such as CXL memory.
